Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Axen Studio's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and massage therapy services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Booking details and preferences
  • Health information relevant to massage therapy
  • Website usage data through Google Analytics (if cookies are accepted)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our massage therapy services
  • Communicate with you about appointments
  • Maintain accurate health records
  • Analyze website performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to analyze traffic and improve your experience. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through our cookie consent banner. If you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site.

5. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your health information is treated with strict confidentiality and stored securely. We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to certain data uses
