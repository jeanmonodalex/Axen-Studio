What is Swedish Massage?

Swedish massage is the most popular traditional form of massage, known for its gentle yet effective approach to promoting relaxation and wellbeing. This technique combines several methods to help you feel your best:

  • Long, flowing strokes (effleurage) to warm up and relax muscles
  • Kneading movements (petrissage) to work deeper into the muscle tissue
  • Circular movements to enhance blood circulation
  • Gentle stretching to improve flexibility

These techniques work together to reduce stress, ease muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote an overall sense of wellbeing. Perfect for both first-time massage recipients and those familiar with bodywork.
